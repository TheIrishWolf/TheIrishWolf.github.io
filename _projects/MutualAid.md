Mutual Aid Networks {#MutualAid}
-------------------
Organizations that focus on community sourced resource distribution.
* [Vancouver Spoon Share](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1396955730605414/)
  * A mutual aid network for 2SLGBTQIA+ disabled people in and around Vancouver.
  * Coordinates
* [Vancouver Community Fridge Project](https://vcfp.square.site/)
  * A project to distribute community fridges and pantries that source inventory from donation.
* [Distro Disco]https://www.instagram.com/distro_disco/
  * A mobile free store that sources inventory from donation.

Means of Production {#MeansofProduction}
-------------------
Organizations that focus on providing access to tools, materials, and the means of production.
* [Vancouver Hack Space](https://vanhack.ca/)
  * A makerspace (community workshop) that provides tools, materials, and community support to make and repair things.
  * Cost is never a barrier, VHS is a nonprofit and membership fees go to rent and upkeep, pay-what-you-can offered.
  * Receives donations which are given for free to members so, long as they aren't resold.
* [vancouver Tool Library](https://vancouvertoollibrary.com/)
  * A community tool library that provides tool loans to members for free.
  * Membership costs operate on a pay-what-you-can model.

Third Spaces {#ThirdSpaces}
------------
Organizations that provide community support, places of encounter, and information resources.
* [Vancouver Black Library](https://www.instagram.com/vanblacklibrary/)
  * A community library providing resources and workspace BIPOC community members.
